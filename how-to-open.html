How to Open a Demat Account?

To open a Demat account, you need to approach a Depository Participant (DP) and complete the required formalities, which usually include submitting KYC documents.

Step Description
1. Choose DP Select a Depository Participant of your choice
2. Submit Documents Provide KYC documents such as identity and address proof
3. Complete Formalities Fill the account opening form and complete in-person verification
